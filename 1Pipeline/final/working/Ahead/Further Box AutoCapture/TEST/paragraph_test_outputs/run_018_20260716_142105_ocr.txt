Because winter roads may remain closed for days, a local controller samples voltage, current temperature, and vibration every 200 milliseconds Detailed records stay on site. while compressed summaries are transmitted whenever the link becomes available Medical and communications loads always receive priority On 18 October 2025, a dust storm reduced solar generation by 34 percent in fifteen minutes. Battery-cabinet temperature increased by 8 °C, and the inverter fan developed an unusual low-frequency vibration Rather than shut down the station, the controller disconnected secondary heaters, reduced selected lighting, and started the backup generator.
